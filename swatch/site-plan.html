Overview

Purpose

[Would you like a personal experience with the wedding planner for your personal event? I would like to give my sister’s start up business a website platform. This site will contain information about Swatch Events and their services and make it appealing through the personalness. The site will provide potential clients with a good understanding of my sister and the effort she will put into her services. The website will give this brand-new company a professional and clean look that instils trust in the clients. There will be ways to contact the business and to select their services.]

Audience

[The primary audience of the service and the site is budget brides. This site would attract those who want low-stress and relatively small-scale receptions and weddings. There generally won't be many other vendors at these events. The style of these events will generally be an LDS style of reception. This is designed to help alleviate stress from the family member or friend who is helping plan the wedding/reception. Even though this is the main audience there are still options for larger-scale events.]
