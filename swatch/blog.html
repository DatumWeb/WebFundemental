Blog and Past Events

Maybe you know you want something amazing, but you are unsure about ideas. I am starting a blog where I talk about different ideas and concepts around events. Here is my first post. There are also my past events at the bottom.

I was married in June 2020 when Covid was running the world. So many things were unsure and my wedding definitely reflects that . It was not at all like the wedding I always dreamed of. Our immediate family were the only people present and we live-streamed the ceremony on Facebook. The weather building up to the big day was consistently bright, warm, and sunny… but it rained for the first time in months on our day...

I want to be there for brides and grooms on their special day. I want to make sure that they aren’t the ones running around getting drenched from the rain of the day. You go enjoy your day and let me handle the rest.
